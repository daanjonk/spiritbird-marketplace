# Shopify Brain — AI-Powered Context for Your Store

Build a personalized AI brain for your Shopify store in 15 minutes. Then use it every day to make smarter decisions.

This plugin does two things:

1. **Setup** — An interactive onboarding that asks the right questions about your store, your customers, and your brand. Based on your answers, it generates a structured vault that gives Claude deep context about YOUR specific business.

2. **Daily Assistant** — A daily driver that keeps your brain alive. Check-ins, weekly reviews, experiment tracking, decision logging. Every session, Claude remembers your business and gets smarter over time.

The result: instead of getting generic AI advice, you get an AI that knows your brand voice, your customer, your channels, and your competitive edge.

## Choose Your Depth

### Foundation (~5 min)
The essentials that make AI actually useful for your store:
Brand voice, ideal customer profile, brand story, marketing channels, tool stack.

### Growth (~12 min)
Everything in Foundation, plus strategic thinking tools:
Competitor analysis, customer segments, experiment tracker, decision log, weekly review.

### Full Brain (~18 min)
The complete setup:
Everything in Growth, plus detailed ad strategy, email flow tracking, SEO planning, support patterns, product templates.

You can always start with Foundation and expand later — just say "expand my brain."

**Already have a brand guide, customer persona, or strategy doc?** Drop it in during setup and Claude will use what's there — less questions for you.

## How to Use

### First Time
After installing, say:

> "Set up my Shopify brain"

Claude will guide you through 5 sections of questions. Some are quick taps, some will make you think about your business in new ways. After each section, Claude writes files to your vault — you'll see it being built in real time.

### Every Session After That
Claude automatically loads your vault context. You don't need to re-explain your business. Just start working.

Some things you can say:

- **"Check in"** — Morning routine. Set your focus for the day.
- **"Weekly review"** — Guided reflection on your week. Numbers, wins, lessons.
- **"New experiment"** — Log something you want to test with a hypothesis and timeline.
- **"Log this decision"** — Capture a decision with reasoning so future-you remembers why.
- **"Catch me up"** — Get a quick briefing on your last session, open experiments, and recent decisions.
- **"Write me ad copy / a product description / an email"** — Claude uses your brand voice and ICP automatically.

## The 5 Setup Sections

1. **Your Store** — The basics: what you sell, how long, solo or team
2. **Your Customer** — Who buys from you and WHY (the deep version)
3. **Your Brand** — How you sound, what you stand for, what you'd never do
4. **Your Channels** — Where your sales come from, what tools you use
5. **Your Edge** — What makes you different, what you know that others don't

## After Setup

Your vault is a living document. The more you use it, the smarter your AI gets:

- Do weekly reviews to track what's working
- Log experiments so you remember what you tested and learned
- Log decisions so you remember WHY you made them
- Share new insights — Claude auto-saves them to the right place
