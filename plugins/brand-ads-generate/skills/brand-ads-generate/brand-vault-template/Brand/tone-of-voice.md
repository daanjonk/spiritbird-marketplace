## How we talk

| We do | We don't |
|---|---|
| `<!-- TODO -->` | `<!-- TODO -->` |
| First-person plural ("we") for brand voice | Royal "we" used for distance / formality |
| Short sentences, most under 12 words | Multi-clause "however / moreover / furthermore" |
| Verbs over adjectives | Adjective stacking ("premium high-quality next-level") |
| Lead with the moment, not the product | Lead with the spec sheet |
| Lead with proof (specific numbers, real quotes) | Lead with claims ("the best", "the only") |

## Style defaults

- Sentence case for headlines (not Title Case, not ALL CAPS unless display-styled)
- One exclamation point per piece, max
- Em-dashes are fine; semicolons are usually not
- Numbers as digits (3, not "three") in body copy
